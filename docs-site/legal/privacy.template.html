1. What we collect

  • Account data: your name, email address, and avatar from the identity provider you sign in with (such as Google or GitHub), and the organizations and roles you belong to.
  • Configuration you create: the monitors, targets (URLs/hosts), request settings, notification channels, and status pages you set up. Secret values such as secret request headers and channel credentials are encrypted at rest.
  • Operational data: check results, incidents, and logs the Service produces while running your checks.
  • Payment data: handled by our payment provider Paddle (see below). We receive subscription and invoice details, but we do not collect or store your full card details.
  • Basic usage analytics: aggregate, cookieless visit statistics on our website (we use Simple Analytics, which does not track individuals).

2. How and why we use it

We use this data to provide and operate the Service (run your checks, send alerts, show your dashboards), to support you, to handle billing, to keep the Service secure, and to meet legal obligations. Where the GDPR applies, our legal bases are: performance of our contract with you (providing the Service), our legitimate interests (security, preventing abuse, improving the Service), compliance with legal obligations (for example tax records), and consent where we ask for it.

3. Processors we share data with

We share data only with providers that help us run the Service:

  • Paddle (Paddle.com Market Ltd) as Merchant of Record, for checkout, payments, invoicing, and tax. See Paddle's privacy notice.
  • Identity providers (such as Google or GitHub) for sign-in.
  • Our hosting and infrastructure providers, who store and process data on our behalf.
  • Simple Analytics for cookieless website statistics.

We do not sell your personal data.

4. International transfers

Some providers may process data outside your country, including outside the EEA. Where that happens we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses) so your data keeps an equivalent level of protection.

5. Retention

We keep account and configuration data while your account is active. Check results and logs are retained according to your plan, then aged out. If you delete your account we delete or anonymize your data within a reasonable period, except where we must keep records (for example invoices) to meet legal obligations.

6. Cookies

We use a small number of strictly necessary cookies to keep you signed in and to protect against cross-site request forgery. Our website analytics are cookieless. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.
