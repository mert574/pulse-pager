Cloud pricing

These plans are for Pulse Pager Cloud, our managed hosting. Pulse Pager is open source, so you can also self-host it for free with no plan at all.

You pick a plan by how often you need to check, from every 15 minutes on Free down to every minute on Professional, with Custom for anything faster. We never charge per check, so the page you see is the bill you get.

Want to run it yourself? The source is on GitHub. Self-hosting has no license fee and none of the plan caps below.

How the bill scales

The main thing you pay for is speed. Each plan sets how often your monitors can run, from every 15 minutes on Free down to every minute on Professional (and faster on Custom), because faster checks cost us more to run.

Monitor count and regions climb with the plan for the same reason.

Changing plans

Upgrades take effect immediately: faster checks, higher caps, and more regions become available at once.

A downgrade never silently deletes your data. If your usage is above the lower plan's limits, you are asked to bring it under the limit first (disable or delete monitors, drop extra regions, or slow down monitors that check faster than the new plan allows) before the downgrade applies.
